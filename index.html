At Wells Fargo's hall, our paths aligned,
A planned meeting, yet fate's design.
From casual talks, a connection grew,
A gentle spark, fresh and new.

From hesitant whispers, a love took its flight,
Through hidden msgs and dreams in the night.
Surprise after surpise, with friend's embrace,
Laughter and gifts, finding joy in each space.

Then distance grew, and battles arose,
Though love still lingered, as everyone knows.
A silent longing, tears softly fell,
Until a new chapter, a hopeful spell.

A meeting awaited, a promise held dear,
Her soft voice, a melody, banishing fear.
First glimpse, a smile, a heart's happy beat,

No past regrets, just future bright,
Our story unfolds, bathed in soft light.
A shy confession, then a tender embrace,
No turning back now, finding their place.

Hands intertwined, in silence they walked,
A magical night, where spirits talked.
No sleep, just stories, a bond taking hold,

Love's open declaration, a truth now revealed,
